Coconut Tree Hill

Mirissa, Sri Lanka

An Island Paradise Icon

Coconut Tree Hill is arguably one of the most Instagrammed spots in Sri Lanka, and for good reason. Located in the coastal town of Mirissa, this unique dome-shaped headland is beautifully populated by towering, slender coconut palms that stretch out towards the Indian Ocean.

The contrast between the reddish-brown soil, the vibrant green fronds of the palm trees, and the deep turquoise of the sea creates a breathtaking palette that captures the true essence of a tropical getaway.

It takes about a 10-15 minute walk from the main Mirissa beach to reach this cliff. It's the perfect spot to relax, listen to the crashing waves, and watch an unforgettable Sri Lankan sunset filtering through the palm trees.

Traveler Tips

  • 🌅 Best Time: Sunrise or Sunset for the best lighting and cooler temperatures.
  • 🎟️ Entrance: Entirely free to the public!
  • 👟 Footwear: Wear sandals or shoes with a decent grip, as the red clay path can get slippery.

How to Get There

You can easily walk along the beach from Mirissa's main bay or take a quick Tuk-Tuk ride from any nearby hotel to the access path.
